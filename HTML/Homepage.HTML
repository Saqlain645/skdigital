About Computer Science


A level 2 course designed to get you ready to study one of our unique industry linked Computer Science courses at Level 3.

Within this course you will be given an introduction to some of the key elements of computer science focusing on the uses of technology systems and the

ongoing developments in the online world. You will be introduced to topics ranging from programming and binary, to web development and digital graphics.

You will also showcase your work in a digital portfolio, designed making use of web technologies. This is a next-generation BTEC qualification,

designed to meet the needs of employers and colleges by covering the latest advancements in technology.
